Mind Read in Prox

 

The developers do some baffling things sometimes.
Like secret dialogue and inaccessible scenes, you mean?
Pretty much.
For example, everybody in Prox at the end can have their minds read.
I got to say, Mind Reading looks like a hell of a fun ability.
I shudder to think what you might do with it.
Silence, brat!
The thing is, as you probably already know, you only have Felix and Isaac in the end. They are very definitely not Jupiter Adepts.
The webmistress used a rom of Golden Sun: The Lost Age and Codebreaker codes to give Isaac Mind Read after lighting Mars Lighthouse, in order to peer into the minds of our fellow Proxians...
And it's all been posted up on this page. Why, there's even a bit more insight into mine and Agatio's fates...
Such a pity, but at least Prox was saved in the end...
