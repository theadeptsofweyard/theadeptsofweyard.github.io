Assist Trophies

 

 

What could this be? Oh, yes, it's confirmation of unlocking an Assist Trophy in the game. They work like Poké Balls, except instead of a Pokémon coming out, a videogame character pops out instead. Quite nifty, yes? The character that comes out of the assist trophy will then put on a show before disappearing. It's close enough to a playable character, but not quite there. Still, at least they get cool 3D models, so it's not so bad.

The character who is an Assist Trophy is none other than Isaac. Yes, many of us dreamed of Isaac being a playable character in SSBB and it looks like we at least have him as an Assist Trophy.

And now there is a video of him in action!

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rxNM7nIIGB4

 

Trophy description:

A strong-willed Psynergy adept of the bloodline of an ancient race thought to have been lost with the
downfall of civilization.
He can manipulate earth-based forces and is also a highly skilled swordsman.
He sets out to protect a lighthouse that can release the sealed powers of alchemy.
In Smash Bros., he uses "Move" energy, which creates an energy field that pushes foes away.

 

The trophy is unlocked by playing 200 VS Matches.

 

Music

There is also Golden Sun music in Super Smash Brothers Brawl.

It is titled "Battle Scene/Final Boss (Golden Sun)" and is a remix of battle music from Golden Sun: The Lost Age, using Felix's battle theme and the Doom Dragon battle theme.

You can watch it on Youtube here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xBHVhDLoRx0
