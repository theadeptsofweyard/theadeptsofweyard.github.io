Oven Guide

Ooh, just thinking about ovens make me hungry.
Who doesn't like looking in ovens?
I wonder what people think about us wandering into their houses and peeking in their ovens...
Not to mention raiding their pots and barrels and even their beds...
We're just a regular bunch of kleptomaniacs...
Of course we don't steal their food though. I think. Do stat boosting items count?
So, here's a handy dandy list of oven descriptions from all over Weyard for you.
Some oven descriptions are not available through normal gameplay. What the heck is up with that?
Bilibin's ovens are blocked off by NPCs. One can be interacted with using walk through codes, but not the other.
The oven descriptions however can be found in text dumps. There are two ovens that give you items, and then tell you it is pitch dark inside when you examine them, in Imil and Suhalla.
There are, however, oven descriptions located in the text that perfectly correspond to Imil and Suhalla. Honestly, it's all very strange indeed. Anyway, these inaccessible oven messages are now included on the page.
