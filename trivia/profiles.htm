Isaac Robin Vlad Hans Isaac Isaac
Age: 17
Hometown: Vale
Element: Venus (Earth)
The main hero of the first game. Three years previously, Isaac saw his father be washed away in a storm and met two odd looking strangers. At the beginning of Golden Sun, he goes into Sol Sanctum with his friends, not knowing that he will end up embarking upon a dangerous quest in which he must prevent the lighthouses from being lit and also save Jenna and Kraden. Isaac, however, learns that the world will be destroyed if the lighthouses aren't lit and accompanies Felix and co. to light the final lighthouse.

Age: 15
Hometown: Kalay (born in Contigo)

As a baby, Ivan was handed over to a silk merchant named Hammet and raised in Kalay, never knowing of his background. Mia Mary Sofia Mia Mia Mia
Age: 17
Hometown: Imil
Element: Mercury (Water)
Mia is one of the sole remaining members of the Mercury Clan and tends to the people of Imil, healing them when they are sick. Mia joins up with Isaac and company after that. She is gentle and kind, but hides a sarcastic streak.

Felix Garcia Pavel Félix Felix Felix
Age: 18
Hometown: Vale
Element: Venus (Earth)
Felix was washed away by the Mt. Aleph boulder during Vale's storm, along with his parents and Isaac's father. They were presumed dead for three years, but in reality, the four of them were being held hostage in Prox. Felix returned to Vale with Saturos and Menardi, who took the Elemental Stars and kidnapped Jenna and Kraden in the process. Felix takes over as the main hero in the second game after the Proxians' death and sets out to light the last two lighthouses. He is very protective and will even risk his life to protect those he cares about.

Sheba Sheba Cylia Sole Sara Cosma
Age: 14
Hometown: Lalivero
Element: Jupiter (Wind)
Sheba grew up in Lalivero, worshipped as a Holy Child by its people, due to the fact that as a baby she fell from the sky into Lalivero and was unharmed. She was raised by the Mayor of Lalivero, Faran, and is considered part of his family. Sheba hopes to find out her past while traveling with Felix but does not. It is, however, hinted that she may have fallen from the moon, which was once Anemos, a city that sat next to Contigo. Sheba is initially timid and quiet, but that's understandable when she keeps getting kidnapped (by Babi, and then by Saturos and Menardi), but shows a much happier side in the second game. She can be quite a tease at times.

Piers Picard Piers Piers Piers Aaron
Age: ???
Hometown: Lemuria
Element: Mercury (Water)
Piers is only in the second game and Felix and co. encounter him in Madra, where he has been jailed since the people believe he is Champa. Piers was washed out to sea by the tidal wave. The Black Orb he requires to pilot his ship is stolen and he goes to get it back. Felix and co. help him out and he rewards them by letting them come with him on his ship. Upon returning to Lemuria, the theory that the world is shrinking due to Alchemy is confirmed and they all set off to light the last two lighthouses. Piers stubbornly keeps his age a secret. He can have a bit of a temper when provoked, but is generally mild-mannered.
