Matthew Mut Matt Matt Matthew Matthew
Age: 16
Hometown: Lookout Cabin
Element: Venus (Earth)
Matthew is the son of Isaac and Jenna, and is the silent type. He sets out with Karis and Tyrell to get a Roc feather to repair the broken soarwing, and ends up getting into more than he had bargained for thanks to the intervention of the Tuaparang. After the Grave Eclipse is unleashed, he and his companions travel to the Apollo Sanctum to fire the lens that will put an end to the eclipse.

Age: 16

Tyrell, son of Garet, is loudly outspoken, brash, and not too smart. He is the best friend of Matthew, though the two are really more like brothers. Amiti Harumani Haru Amiti Amiti Jao
Age: Approx. 19
Hometown: Ayuthay
Element: Mercury (Water)
Amiti is the prince of Ayuthay, his uncle being the King. He spent his whole life so far believing that his mother, Veriti, was an Adept and that he had been born from her power. It's only after Matthew and company arrive in Ayuthay- he had a father, a powerful Adept whose identity was unknown and who started up the Alchemy Well. It is strongly implied ingame, and eventually confirmed by Word of God, that this Adept is Alex himself, but Amiti has yet to find out. He strongly dislikes criminals and believes the Champan pirates to be no good until he ends up owing his life to them.

Age: ?
Hometown: Belinsk
Element: Jupiter (Wind)
Sveta is a manbeast Adept, belonging to a race that mutated after the formation of the Golden Sun. She is a member of the Czamaral clan, the royal family of Morgal, and sister to Volechek, the king. Sveta first meets Matthew and co. in Tepe Ruins but after helping them through, she leaves again and later returns to help them rescue Eoleo and Hou Ju, who are being held captive in the Belinsk castle. She is forced to leave after things go wrong and remains with Matthew and company.
