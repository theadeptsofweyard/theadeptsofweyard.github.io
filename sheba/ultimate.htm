Golden Sun has returned to the Smash series after only getting a new music track in the previous Smash installment with no assist trophy in sight. First off is the return of the Isaac assist trophy.

Official video with the reveal of the Isaac assist trophy is below.

 

There is a feature in Smash Ultimate where you can call spirits, represented by static official artwork. Isaac, Garet, Ivan, Mia, Felix, Jenna, adult Isaac from Dark Dawn and Matthew are all confirmed to be spirits.

Both Golden Sun tracks to have been included in Smash in the past return. There are no new Golden Sun related music tracks.

The Mii Fighter can be dressed up in a variety of costumes. One of the available costumes is themed after Isaac.
