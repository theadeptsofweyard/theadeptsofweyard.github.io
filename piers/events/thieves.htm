Thieves Event

 

Thieves? Oh wait, those guys, right?
Yup, those guys. They heard us mention Isaac...
And assumed it was the same Isaac they knew.
Well, it was...
It's like there's only one Isaac in the whole of Weyard.
I troubled you guys quite a bit, didn't I? Sorry...
Eh, it's not your fault they decided to jump random people who knew you.
How did they even get all the way over to Indra?
That's a good question. Probably the same way as Feizhi...

 

In Golden Sun, you defeat some thieves who stole the Shaman's Rod from Hammet. They get thrown into Vault's jail. After leaving the Lamakan Desert, you can visit Vault and upon speaking to the mayor of Vault, you will learn that the thieves have escaped from jail.

If you transfer data to The Lost Age, you will encounter the thieves after returning to Madra with Piers, speaking to the mayor, and seeing the scene with Karst then leaving Madra. The thieves will have overheard you mentioning Isaac and attack you. After the battle, they will give you the Golden Boots, then flee.
