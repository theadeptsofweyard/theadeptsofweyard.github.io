Well, anyway, let's explain the Feizhi event.


The Feizhi event requires you to have saved Hsu at Alpine Crossing when he's been trapped by boulders.

If you do this and transfer data, a cutscene will trigger upon entering Champa. Feizhi will turn up and mistake you for Isaac. Kraden teases her over having a crush on Isaac, and she ends up giving Felix the Golden Ring, which is a good luck charm for Isaac.
