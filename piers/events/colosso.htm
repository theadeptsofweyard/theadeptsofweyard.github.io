In Golden Sun, you will enter Colosso and fight Satrage, Azart and Navampa. If you beat all three of them and transfer data to Golden Sun: The Lost Age, you will unlock an event in Shaman Village Cave, where the three of them accuse you of cheating in Colosso and attack you. Upon defeating them, they will present you with the Golden Shirt.
