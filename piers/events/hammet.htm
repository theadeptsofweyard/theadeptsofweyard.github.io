In Golden Sun, Ivan's master, Hammet, gets captured after going to Lunpa. You learn of the situation after visiting Kalay, but it isn't until you get Babi's Cloak Ball in Tolbi that you are finally able to do something about this. This unlocks an optional sidequest that involves infilitrating Lunpa Fortress and rescuing Hammet from Dodonpa's clutches. In Golden Sun: The Lost Age, after the reunion and going to the now winged ship, Hama will tell you that she visited Kalay and brought you a gift from Hammet, an Orihalcon.
