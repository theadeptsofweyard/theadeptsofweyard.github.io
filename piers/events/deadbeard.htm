Well, anecdotes aside, let's explain this event...

 

In Golden Sun, you get to access this optional dungeon on Crossbone Isle.

At the very end, all the way down, you get to a ship and encounter the secret super boss... Deadbeard.

If you beat Deadbeard and transfer your password to The Lost Age, you unlock a small event which does not give you any items. At some point during The Lost Age, possibly after Briggs has fled Alhafra, if you go to the top floor of Alhafra Inn, you will find some pirates talking about Isaac, who is apparently now the leader of theb pirates after defeating Deadbeard.
