Names in the Japanese Version

  Names in the Japanese version, huh?
Some names do differ... so I guess it's interesting.
Or the webmistress needs to spend more time on her fanfiction than this.
Yeah, well... that's true...
Anyway, these are mostly names that are available in the debug room.
Or some Kyarorain just happened to know.
So, enjoy looking at her dodgy romanisation skills!
There are also town names. And, it seems Izumo's continent has a name according to a Japanese map on Camelot's site. So that's where she got the name.
My father's Japanese name can be written differently, right?
Kyarorain thinks it's funny. So, she won't write it differently. It's an accurate romanisation anyhow.
